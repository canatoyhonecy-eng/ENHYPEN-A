About ENHYPEN

Welcome to the Universe of ENHYPEN

Formed through the survival competition show I-LAND under BELIFT LAB (a joint venture between CJ ENM and HYBE), ENHYPEN debuted on November 30, 2020. The name signifies connection, discovery, and growth, proving how seven different elements link up to create something incredible.

As ENGENEs, you are the engine that drives their music, concepts, and worldwide milestones. From "Given-Taken" to their latest eras, the journey of these seven members continues to reshape global music boundaries.
